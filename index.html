Tips & Tricks

1. Start Early

Start your assignments as soon as possible. Avoid last-minute rush to ensure quality and meet the deadline.

2. Stay Organized

Break down the assignment into smaller parts and complete them one by one to stay organized and on track.

3. Refer to Study Materials

Use the provided IGNOU study materials and other resources to answer the questions thoroughly.

4. Take Breaks

Avoid burnout by taking short breaks between study sessions. It helps you stay focused and fresh.

Assignment Writing Rules

  1. Write all answers in your own handwriting on A4 size sheets.
  2. Leave a margin on the left side of each page for comments.
  3. Include your Enrollment Number, Course Code, and Study Center at the top of every assignment.
  4. Ensure that the answers are well-structured and written neatly.
  5. Write your name, roll number, and address on the first page of the assignment.
